About Me

Rhonda is a technical product manager with full-stack web development skills and entrepreneurial ambitions. During her 15 years in the software industry, she has worked in developer relations, product management, and software documentation.

Rhonda has a pilot's license, as well as an instrument ground instructor certificate, and enjoys sightseeing, taking aerial photographs, and writing trip reports.

She holds a Master of Science in geoscience from Western Kentucky University and a bachelor ºs degree in political science. In April 2018, she completed an immersive, full-stack web development bootcamp at UC Berkeley Extension.
